The Recipe Search tab allows you to specify parameters to locate recipes you want to view or share.
The fields and buttons are:   Here you specify which collection of recipes you want to search.
Personal Recipes are just the recipes you have added yourself.
Shared Recipes are recipes that you have shared or others have shared that you have access to.
  These collections must be searched separately. The internal values of the Origins and Categories in the shared collection are different from those in any personal collection.   Here you specify any categories that apply to recipes of interest. A recipe must have ALL the specified categories to be selected.
  Click the button to display a menu of categories. Add categories to the search by checking any you want to apply and click . Selected categories will appear in the categories field as labeled chips.
  To remove a selection click the on the chip with that category label.   This field allows you to restrict the search to a particular origin.
Click the field and select an entry from the list.   Here you can specify a list of words or phrases to search for in the Title and Ingredients fields of the recipes. Enter words or phrases separated by commas.
  For example:
coco,baking powder,Crisco
will select recipes whos Title or Ingredients fields contain the words coco or Crisco or the phrase baking powder.   Here you can limit the search to recipes with Origin dates before, after or between certain dates.
  To find recipes dated on or since a given year, enter that year in the first date field.
  To find recipes dated on or before a given year, enter that year in the second date field.
  Press this button to submit your search request. If successful, the Recipes tab will be displayed. If no matches meeting the parameters are found, a message to that effect will be given and the Recipe Search form will remain displayed.   If a search continues to find no matches, make sure the parameters you are specifying are not too restrictive. ALL restrictions you specify must be satisfied for a recipe to be selected. The more information you enter into this form, the more restricted your search will become.
  For example, if you are looking for a recipe for Saffron Chicken, specifying the Category 'Chicken' and the keyword 'saffron' should do the trick.
