The Delete Account form allows you to delete your account.
The fields and buttons are:   This is the email that is associated with your account. The value is filled in by the program.   Enter the password associated with this account.   Press this button to submit your delete request. You will immediately be prompted for confirmation of this action. Press Delete Account to continue. If the delete issuccessful, you will receive a brief notification and the user icon at the top of the page will become an outline.
